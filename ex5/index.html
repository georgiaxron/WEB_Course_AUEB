Digital Forensics
  • Applications
  • Career
Digital forensics is a very fresh and highly technical branch of forensic science that describes the procedures of the recovery and investigation of material found in digital devices. It is responsible for the investigation of all devices capable of storing digital data, even cloud storage. Digital forensics has several categories regarding what type of digital device is involved in an investigation; computer forensics, network forensics, forensic data analysis, and mobile device forensics.
